What is Zoho Books?

Zoho Books is a cloud accounting system that centralizes income, expenses, cash flow, and financial processes in one place. It automates reconciliations, invoicing and taxes, ensures regulatory compliance, and allows you to obtain clear reports and accurate projections to make better decisions.
