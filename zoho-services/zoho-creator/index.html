What is Zoho Creator?

Zoho Creator is a low-code development platform that allows you to create custom business applications to automate processes, manage data, and connect business areas without advanced programming skills.
With the support of InterConnecta, each application is designed to support your real workflows, business rules, and growth objectives.

Real use cases with Zoho Creator

Internal project management

Task tracking, milestones, responsibilities, and resources in custom applications for your teams.

Inventory and logistics control

Record entries and exits, traceability, purchase orders, and real-time delivery statuses.

Customer or supplier portals

Secure self-service access to orders, invoices, requests, or specific documentation.

Approval automation

Configurable workflows for HR, finance, or sales: approvals for vacations, expenses, discounts, contracts, and more.

Custom mobile applications

Apps created once and available on any device, for field, operations, or sales teams.

Enterprise integrations

Connect Creator with APIs and external systems in a few steps to build an integrated architecture.
