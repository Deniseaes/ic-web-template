Zoho for Education & Training

We help educational institutions, training centers, and learning organizations organize students, academic processes, operations, and programs within a connected, clear, and easy-to-manage ecosystem.

Request information

Educational management requires operational clarity and a unified view of students, programs, and processes.

When information lives in disconnected tools, it becomes difficult to coordinate admissions, classes, assessments, payments, and communications. This affects academic continuity and the overall institutional experience.
With Zoho, every process is centralized in a single platform: from recruitment and enrollment to academic progress, certification, payments, and support.

Our approach

We combine technology, processes, and educational expertise to help you operate with more order, agility, and visibility. Our framework integrates:

Centralized management of students, programs, and cohorts

Automation of admissions, enrollment, and communications

Clear academic flows for courses, classes, and instructors

Coordination between administrative and academic areas

Control of payments, scholarships, registrations, and attendance

Integration with educational platforms, LMS, and internal systems

Analytics to understand demand, retention, and performance

The result: a more streamlined operation and a more consistent experience for students and teams.
