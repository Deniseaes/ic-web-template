Automation of inbound, outbound, and transfer transactions

Solutions that transform every inventory area

Receiving / Inbound

  • Recording of incoming goods without manual spreadsheets.
  • Quantity and lot verification.
  • Immediate labeling for full traceability.
  • Integration with purchase orders.

Storage / Warehouse Control

  • Exact location of products and materials.
  • Control by batch, serial number, or expiration date.
  • Internal transfers with full traceability.
  • Digital audits and cycle counts.

Picking & Packing

  • Tasks automatically assigned by zone or warehouse.
  • Optimized picking by route or priority.
  • Validation before dispatch.
  • Fewer errors when preparing orders.

Dispatch / Outbound

  • Order control from warehouse to delivery.
  • Integration with logistics and transportation systems.
  • Recording of incidents or returns.
  • Digital delivery confirmation.

Purchasing / Procurement

  • Automated replenishment based on minimum stock and demand.
  • Control of suppliers and lead times.
  • Comparison of prices and conditions.
  • Purchase orders generated without manual intervention.

Customer Service

  • Visibility into order status and available inventory.
  • Tickets for discrepancies, returns, or shortages.
  • Portal for customers or distributors.

Business Intelligence & Analytics

  • Critical KPIs: turnover, critical stock, stockouts, lead times, accuracy.
  • Real-time executive dashboards.
  • Predictive models for demand and replenishment.
