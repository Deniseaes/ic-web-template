Sustainability & ESG

Home About Us Sustainability & ESG

Global innovation, responsible impact.

At InterConnecta, we believe that technology only makes sense when it creates value for people, organizations, and the planet. That’s why we integrate sustainability, inclusion, and governance criteria into the way we operate and support our global clients.

Our global and hybrid model combines remote collaboration with strategic on-site instances: we reduce travel, optimize resources, and strengthen security and transparency in every project.

  • Environmental efficiency through cloud and paperless practices
  • Diversity and professional development in distributed teams
  • Governance and compliance (privacy and security)
  • Impact measurement and continuous improvement

Our ESG Pillars

01.

Environmental — Efficiency and Sustainability

We combine remote operations with planned on-site meetings to minimize emissions and resource usage. We prioritize cloud solutions, automation, and paperless workflows.

  • Reduction of non-essential travel
  • Cloud optimization and energy-efficient environments
  • Green IT best practices in projects
02.

Social — Diversity, Wellbeing, and Development

Distributed teams across multiple countries with equal opportunities, flexible work arrangements, and continuous learning through IC Academy.

  • Inclusion and equity policies
  • Upskilling/reskilling programs
  • Work-life balance in hybrid environments
03.

Governance — Ethics, Security, and Transparency

Governance based on data and auditable processes. Compliance with international privacy and information security regulations, aligned with our partners' standards.

  • GDPR compliance and privacy policies
  • Documented and traceable processes
  • Risk management and internal controls

Global Impact (Key Indicators)

We measure our progress to ensure continuous improvement in sustainability and culture.

  • Operations in 10+ countries with hybrid teams
  • 40% fewer trips on average in projects (vs. historical baseline)
  • Active training and equity programs in all regions
