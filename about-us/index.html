Our History:

From enterprise experience to global innovation.
Since 2009, InterConnecta has been guided by the vision of its founder and CEO, Luis González, along with a team with more than 15 years of experience in CRM and enterprise technology.

That experience gave us a deep understanding of how world-class enterprise solutions are structured and scaled.
