Careers at InterConnecta

At InterConnecta, we help organizations optimize their processes and operations through the strategic use of technology. We operate with remote, collaborative teams and deliver services to clients across multiple industries and regions.

We are looking for professionals with strong analytical skills, execution capabilities, and autonomy, interested in developing their careers in a technical, demanding, and multicultural environment.

Our Standard

At InterConnecta, we seek professionals who meet the following general criteria:

Advanced proficiency in English and Spanish.

Experience in technology-driven and enterprise environments.

Ability to work autonomously and in a structured way, focused on results.

Experience collaborating with multidisciplinary and distributed teams.

Analytical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Clear communication and well-organized documentation.

Continuous learning mindset and openness to feedback.

Alignment with a horizontal, ethical, and trust-based culture.

(Specific requirements vary depending on the position.)

Permanent Recruitment Positions

Responsible for the analysis of business processes and the gathering of functional requirements, acting as a liaison between client teams and internal technical teams. This role involves eliciting, documenting and analyzing operational processes, translating business requirements into functional configurations and technical tasks within the Zoho ecosystem,following established best practices and guidelines. Works closely with developers, project managers and commercial teams, producing functional documentation, process flow diagrams and requirement summaries, and ensuring clearcommunication among all stakeholders throughout the project lifecycle.

Responsible for the development, customization and integration of solutions on the Zoho platform, leveraging standard functionalities, automations and custom developments based on project requirements. The role includes building custom functions, workflow rules and integrations with third-party systems, ensuring the reliability, performance and maintainability of implemented solutions. Collaborates with functional analysts, project managers and other developers, contributing to continuous improvement of solution architectures and effective resolution of technical issues.

Responsible for the design, implementation and evolution of advanced solutions on the Zoho platform, providing technical leadership and accountability for delivery quality. The role encompasses architecture definition, development of complex automations, API-based integrations and ownership of the full development lifecycle, from design through production deployment and ongoing support. Additionally, provides technical guidance to other team members, contributes to development roadmaps, documents solutions and supports the standardization of technical best practices across the organization.

Responsible for the planning, execution, monitoring and control of Zoho implementation projects, ensuring compliance with scope, schedule, budget and defined quality standards. This role involves coordinating internal resources, assigning and prioritizing tasks, continuously tracking project progress and communicating status updates to clients and key stakeholders. Works closely with technical teams, functional analysts, account executives and clients, managing risks, escalating issues when necessary and driving continuous improvement in projectmanagement processes.

Responsible for managing the full sales lifecycle, from prospecting and opportunity qualification through deal closure and long-term client relationship management. The role requires a strong understanding of client business processes to identify opportunities for Zoho solution implementation and expansion, while coordinating closely with marketing anddelivery teams. Additionally, maintains accurate commercial records within the CRM, prepares proposals and quotations, participates in negotiations and contributes to the sustained growth of the client portfolioacross multiple industries.

Responsible for the execution of daily accounting operations, including accounts receivable, accounts payable, invoicing, collections and vendor payments. The role includes account and tax analysis, application of withholdings and deductions, as well as support for payroll processing and monthly, quarterly and annual financial close activities. Also contributes to financial reporting, regional accounting consolidation and preparation of financial statements, working closely with finance and operations teams.

Responsible for the end-to-end management of human resources processes, including recruitment, compensation, organizational development, performance management, culture and employee relations. This role involves designing and executing talent acquisition and development strategies, advising leaders on people management matters, and analyzing indicators related to performance, engagement and retention. Works cross-functionally with all departments, supporting organizational change initiatives, leadership development and alignment between talent strategy and business objectives.
