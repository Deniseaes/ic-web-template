Careers at InterConnecta

At InterConnecta, we help organizations optimize their processes and operations through the strategic use of technology. We operate with remote, collaborative teams and deliver services to clients across multiple industries and regions.

We are looking for professionals with strong analytical skills, execution capabilities, and autonomy, interested in developing their careers in a technical, demanding, and multicultural environment.

Our Standard

At InterConnecta, we seek professionals who meet the following general criteria:

Advanced proficiency in English and Spanish.

Experience in technology-driven and enterprise environments.

Ability to work autonomously and in a structured way, focused on results.

Experience collaborating with multidisciplinary and distributed teams.

Analytical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Clear communication and well-organized documentation.

Continuous learning mindset and openness to feedback.

Alignment with a horizontal, ethical, and trust-based culture.

(Specific requirements vary depending on the position.)
